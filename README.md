AdalatMitra is an AI-powered legal assistance platform designed to simplify access to legal information and court case tracking for citizens, lawyers, and legal professionals.

The platform integrates real-time court data, AI-powered legal explanations, multilingual support, document analysis, and intelligent case timeline tracking to help users better understand their legal matters.

Key Features:
• Court Case Tracking
• Case Timeline Analysis
• AI-Powered Legal Explanations
• Multilingual Support
• Legal Notice & Document Understanding
• Bharat Courts Integration
• Agent-Based AI Architecture
• Gemini & Groq LLM Integration
• Future RAG & Legal Knowledge Base Support

Tech Stack:
Python, Streamlit, FastAPI, Gemini, Groq, LangGraph, Qdrant, RAG, OCR, Bharat Courts API

Vision:
To make legal information accessible, understandable, and actionable for every citizen through Artificial Intelligence.
